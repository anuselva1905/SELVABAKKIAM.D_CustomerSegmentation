Customer Segmentation App

This application performs RFM (Recency, Frequency, Monetary Value) analysis on customer transaction data to segment customers into meaningful groups.

About RFM Analysis

RFM analysis is a marketing technique used to determine customers' value based on:

  • Recency (R): How recently a customer made a purchase
  • Frequency (F): How often they make purchases
  • Monetary Value (M): How much money they spend

The app will analyze your data and classify customers into segments like Champions, Loyal Customers, At Risk, etc.
